Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Bulk TikTok Video Downloader extension handles your information. We keep it simple, transparent, and focused on protecting your privacy.

Product: Bulk TikTok Video Downloader

Overview

Bulk TikTok Video Downloader is designed to help users download publicly available TikTok videos that they have permission to save. The extension operates only when the user actively interacts with it.

What Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • No names, emails, or personal identifiers
  • No browsing history tracking
  • No analytics or tracking scripts

Website Content Access

The extension may access publicly available content on TikTok pages (such as video URLs) only when the user activates the extension. This is required to enable downloading functionality.

This data is processed temporarily and is never stored or transmitted for tracking purposes.

Permissions and Why We Use Them

  • activeTab — Allows the extension to interact with the current TikTok page only when the user clicks it.
  • downloads — Required to save video files to the user's device.
  • storage — Used to store basic user preferences locally, if applicable.

How the Extension Works

When you visit a TikTok page and activate the extension, it detects publicly available video content and provides download options. Downloads are only initiated through user action.

Third-Party Services

The extension may interact with third-party services, such as video processing APIs, only when the user requests a download.

These services are used solely to process publicly available video content and are not used for tracking, profiling, or data collection.

Cookies and Local Storage

This extension does not use cookies. It may use chrome.storage to store basic preferences locally. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Data Retention

Since no personal data is collected, there is no external data storage. Any locally stored preferences remain in your browser until you clear them or uninstall the extension.

Security

All functionality is executed within Chrome’s secure extension environment. The extension does not execute remote code and does not expose users to unnecessary security risks.

User Control

You are always in control. You can enable, disable, or uninstall the extension at any time through your browser settings.

Compliance Statement

This extension does not attempt to access private, restricted, or protected content. It only works with publicly accessible content and respects platform rules and user permissions.

Children

This extension is intended for general use and is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.
